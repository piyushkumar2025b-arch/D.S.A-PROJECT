# 🚧 4. Section 3: Research Problems

**Section 3** is the interactive demonstrator outlining exactly what limits exist when using AI agents, and how SAAP attempts to fix them.

### ⚙️ How to Use It
1. Click into Section 3.
2. You will be presented with a Dashboard consisting of major known "Agentic Failures" (e.g., Infinite Loops, Deadlocks, Context Fragmentation).
3. **Interaction**:
   - Select a specific research problem from the UI panel.
   - Click the "Simulate" or "Analyze" button attached to the problem scope.
   - The UI will actively trigger a simulated failure environment demonstrating how two Sub-Agents might crash against each other natively.
   - Finally, SAAP will deploy its mitigation wrapper, proving how the orchestration fixes the problem.

### 🧠 Core Concept
This functions as the "Whitepaper" module of your application, validating that your AI architecture isn't just theoretical, but actively defends against runtime threats.
